09 Juli

17 Ramadan 1443 H

Eid al-Adha 1443 H

Eid al-Adha is commonly called "Eid of Hajj", because it is at that time that Muslims generally perform the Hajj. After the Eid prayer, the sacrificial animal is usually slaughtered, and the meat from the slaughter is then distributed to residents in the area concerned or to residents who are less fortunate. During Eid al-Adha, the Muslim community also performs Eid prayers.
